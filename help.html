Things you can do

Carpool

Share your ride(s) with your fellow co-worker(s), take the bus, use your bycicle or better yet, walk!

Use less energy

Turn off the lights and the television when you are not using them. Instead of using the dryer, hang your clothes to dry. Unplug your laptop or anything that you are not using.

Recycle, recycle and recycle!

Recycle everything that can possibly be recycled in your household.

When you see trash on the ground, securely put it where it belongs.

Michelle Cantin
Eat organic and local

By eating organic and local, you are discouraging transportation and the use of pestisides and chemicals.

Buy energy efficient

When buying electronics and appliances, make sure that they are energy efficient so as to use less electricity.
